# Gas fees

For every transaction you do on the blockchain, you have to pay a `transaction fee`. The txn fee on solana is constant (5000 lamport per signature in the txn).

**SOL Burn Mechanism**

*If there is enough activity on the blockchain, more and more SOL gets burned — making it deflationary.*

**How It Works**

1. **Transaction Occurs** — Every transaction on Solana costs a small base fee of 5,000 lamports.
2. **50% of the Base Fee Is Burned** — Permanently removed from the circulating SOL supply.
3. **Supply Decreases Over Time** — More activity = more burns = deflationary pressure on SOL.
